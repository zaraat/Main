Our app is designed to help farmers from growing crop to selling crop in the market. a complete solution for the farmers.

App Features

Below are listed some of the Key features of our application

Schedule Soil testing

With the help of our app farmers can schedule a soil test in seconds.

Crop Planner

Farmers can now plan things ahead with the help of our app.

Weather Alerts

With the help of the most accurate weather API's our app gives farmer weather updates in an instant.

Access to Quality tools/inputs

With the help of our app farmers can get quality inputs and tools when they require.

Talk to experts

When in need farmers can connect to our experts and get help with their farming related queries.

Sell Produce

Farmers can now sell produce directly to institutional buyers.
